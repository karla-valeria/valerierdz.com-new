meet your designer

DESIGN GROUNDED IN STRUCTURE AND CREATIVITY

Hello, my name is Valerie. I’m a Phoenix-based graphic designer with a background in computer science and fine art. My work combines structured thinking with strong visual direction, resulting in clear, intentional design systems across branding, print, and digital.
I began as a fine artist working in color and illustration, later developing a technical foundation through web and software development. Today, I bring both perspectives into my design practice, balancing creativity with precision and strategic clarity.

My Design Approach

Detail-oriented and highly organized, I approach design with intention, consistency, and a strong sense of visual curation. I focus on creating versatile, modern design solutions that are both aesthetically refined and practically effective.
