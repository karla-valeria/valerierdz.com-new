meet your designer

DESIGN GROUNDED IN STRUCTURE AND CREATIVITY

Hello, my name is Valerie. I’m a Phoenix-based graphic designer with a background in computer science and fine art. My work combines structured thinking with strong visual direction, resulting in aesthetic and strategic design systems across branding, print, and digital.

I began as a fine artist working in color and illustration, later developing a technical foundation through web and software development. Today, I bring both perspectives into my design practice, balancing creativity with precision.

My Design Approach

As a detail-oriented and highly organized designer, I prioritize intention, consistency, and visual curation in my work. I focus on creating versatile design solutions that are both aesthetically refined and practically effective for modern businesses and creators.
